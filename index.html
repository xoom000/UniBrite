Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How do you get paid?

We work on a performance basis. You only pay us a percentage of what we save you. If we don't reduce your costs, you don't pay anything. It's zero risk to you. Typical fee is 25-30% of first year savings, and you keep 100% of the savings after that forever.

Will this damage my relationship with my current provider?

No. We handle all negotiations professionally and directly. Providers expect this - it's business. They'd rather keep you as a client at a fair rate than lose you entirely. We've renegotiated hundreds of contracts with Cintas, Aramark, UniFirst, and Vestis. The relationship remains intact.

How long does this take?

Typical timeline is 4-6 weeks from contract analysis to signed new agreement. We do 90% of the work - you just review and approve the final terms. Most clients spend less than 2 hours total on the entire process.

What if I'm in the middle of a contract?

Perfect time to start. We can often renegotiate existing terms even mid-contract, or we'll prepare for your renewal date so you're ready. The earlier we start, the more leverage you have and the more we can save.

Do you work with all uniform providers?

Yes. We've successfully negotiated with Cintas, Aramark, UniFirst, Vestis, and virtually every other uniform rental provider. We know their pricing models, contract structures, and negotiation tactics inside and out.

What size businesses do you work with?

We work with everyone from single-location businesses to multi-state enterprises. If you're spending $1,000+ per month on uniforms, we can likely help. Our largest client saves $380,000 annually. Our smallest saves $14,000. Both got similar percentage reductions.
